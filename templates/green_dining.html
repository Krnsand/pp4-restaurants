Welcome to Green Dining

With a passion for local and sustainably sourced food


The wild life in the fine dining

We here at Green Dining have a real passion for food. We also have a passion for animals and how they are kept and handled. The three of us have been friends since we were young and we have all always preferred to hunt for the meat we want instead of buying it from a store. Now, we understand that might not be for everyone, but that doesn’t mean they shouldn’t be allowed to eat like it! We do also have vegetarian options as well as fish as fresh as can be for those not so meat enthusiasts out there who still want to take part in this dining experience.

  • Seared duck served with roasted baby potatoes and a delight sauce with gooseberry and tarragon
  • Fillet of red deer with mushroom croquettes, heirloom carrots and root celery emulsion
  • Fish of the day served with stewed spinach, confit potatoes and a light lemony butter sauce

Why not come on by and try the wild life in a fine dining environment. Or grab a beer at the bar with Jamie the house hunter/chef when he peaks out of the kitchen for some laughs.
