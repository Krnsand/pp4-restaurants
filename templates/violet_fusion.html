Welcome to Violet Fusion

Violet Fusion, your new go-to place

Violet Fusion is all about, you guessed it, fusion foods. We have been all over the world and can't get enough of mouth watering scents, memory creating tastes and unforgettable experiences. So although we can’t bring all the wonders of the world right to your table, we can, and will, do our best at recreating our favorites from around the world in a new contemporary way. Some of our stand out dishes are:

  • Mango & Scallop ceviche
  • Lobster sallad velit.
  • Portobello dumplings in our secret sauce

It doesn’t matter if you want a 7 course meal in our coxy but stylish dinner hall, or if you want to drink champagne in our garden. You and your party are welcome to try on flavors from worlds apart.
